-	Jonte5s: A five second interval where hand goes from relaxed state to cliched and back to relaxed every 5 second. 
        The duration of the hand movement is approximately 2 seconds.

-	Jonte10s: A ten second interval where the state of the hand goes from 10 seconds relaxed to 10 seconds clinched. Switching 
        between the states goes on for 2 min. 

-	Jonteimaginary: The same as jonte10s but with no physical movement. Imaginary movement of relaxed and closed hand.

-	JonteRest:  A 2-minute sample under full relaxation.
